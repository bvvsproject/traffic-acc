About TrafficAI

An advanced machine learning initiative aimed at improving road safety through predictive analytics. By analyzing historical accident data, we empower emergency responders and urban planners with intelligent insights.

🎯 Core Objectives

  • ❖ Analyze extensive historical traffic accident datasets for actionable patterns.
  • ❖ Identify critical environmental and infrastructure factors affecting severity.
  • ❖ Deploy high-accuracy Decision Tree models for real-time risk assessment.
  • ❖ Classify incidents across a 4-tier severity spectrum automatically.
  • ❖ Assist in accelerating emergency response and improving road safety policies.
